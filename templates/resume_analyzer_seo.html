Frequently Asked Questions

What is an ATS score?

An ATS (Applicant Tracking System) score measures how compatible your resume is with automated hiring systems. Scores range from 0-100, with higher scores indicating better ATS compatibility.

Yes, we process your resume securely and don't store personal information. All analysis is done in real-time.
